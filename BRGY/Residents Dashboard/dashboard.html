Types of Certificate Available

BARANGAY
CLEARANCE

This certificate is often used for verifying character and good moral conduct, especially when applying for jobs, loans, or school admissions.

CERTIFICATE OF INDIGENCY

This certificate is often required when applying for government assistance programs, medical benefits, or educational scholarships.

CERTIFICATE OF RESIDENCY

This certificate is often required for job applications, school enrollments, and various legal and administrative processes such as applying for government IDs, opening bank accounts, and registering to vote.

BARANGAY BUSINESS Certificate

This permit ensures that the business complies with local regulations and ordinances. It is often a prerequisite for obtaining a city or municipal business permit and for renewing other business-related licenses.
